    Calculation breakdown

    Raw field sums (hours over the period)

    Sums of every Hrs_* field returned by CMS, summed across all days in the range. The two greyed-out rows are excluded from every metric below per your definitions.

    Census denominator

    HPRD = (sum of staff hours) ÷ (sum of MDScensus across all days). Per CMS methodology, the denominator is the total resident-days, not the average daily census.

    Per-metric arithmetic

    Daily detail
    Extended-timeframe staffing reports built from the CMS Payroll-Based Journal Daily Nurse Staffing public data. Reports are generated from quarterly public datasets; the most recent quarter lags real time.
